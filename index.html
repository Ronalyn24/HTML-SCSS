    HOW WE OPERATE

We combine IQ and EQ to help our portfolio companies scale. Founders and funds come to us for our CPG expertise, extensive global network, and our straightforward and supportive style.

We believe that leading CPG brands will continue to need to "buy-in" innovation through strategic acquisitions. We focus on investing in new companies that cover the blind spots of industry incumbents, bringing our buyers'-side experience to the builders.

We're collaborative. We believe that success isn't a zero-sum game. We strive to connect with great founders, operators and investors and win as a team.

Anna-Lena Kamenetzky

  • INVESTMENT TEAM
  • Linkedin

Anna-Lena is a proven business builder and leader with two decades of global experience spanning the U.S., Europe, and Asia. Since 2020, she has focused her expertise on identifying, investing in, and supporting start-up companies that address innovation gaps in the portfolios of incumbent players in the consumer goods and services industry. She concentrates on consumer trends in digitization as well as better-for-you, more sustainable and authentic brands.

Anna-Lena was one of eight partners at JAB Holding Company where she was instrumental in developing and executing JAB's global coffee and non-alcoholic beverage strategy, resulting in the global #4 in non-alcoholic beverages and global #2 in

coffee. In her role co-leading the JAB Consumer Fund, Anna-Lena was elemental in raising and investing an additional $17 billion from some of the world's most respected investors. She has served on numerous boards across three continents, including Keurig Dr. Pepper, Jacobs Douwe Egberts, Jimmy Choo, and Coty. Her prior experience includes Managing Director of Ripplewood Holdings Japan International and Vice President at Goldman Sachs.

Anna-Lena is fluent in English and German and enjoys the occasional conversation in French (Test her, please!). She is a mother of two kids and two labradoodles.

Grace
Gould

  • INVESTMENT TEAM
  • Linkedin

Grace is an experienced early-stage investor and operator with experience in the U.K. and the U.S. She launched her career at Apple in London where, among other things, she trained as an Apple Genius. She then went to work for consumer electronics supply chain management group PCH International, based in Shenzhen and San Francisco. Grace applied her consumer technology experience to launch the venture-backed School of the Digital Age, a retail concept for new technology products in partnership with companies such as Selfridges, Neiman Marcus, and Nordstrom.

On the investment side, she has worked on the early-stage investment team at Index Ventures in London and at an investment

advisory group focused on the business ventures of leading global entertainers and athletes.

Grace's operational experience encompasses supply chain management, physical product development, retail partnerships, and go-to-market strategy. In addition to her work, she has been an advisor to the U.K. Government's Digital Industrialisation Strategy and the IPPR Commission on Economic Justice.

Grace currently lives in New York City. Outside of the office she loves gathering friends and family for dinner parties, trying out new CPG products, and devouring a host of industry and non-industry newsletters.

Claudia Romo Edelman

  • ADVISORY TEAM
  • Linkedin

Claudia is a marketing expert and brand builder with more than 25 years of experience. She has worked for the World Economic Forum and was part of the team that elevated Davos to a globally recognized and aspirational brand.

A former diplomat and journalist, Claudia's extensive network includes high-level decision makers and leaders from all sectors, most notably in the Latin American and U.S. Hispanic Community. She is an expert on environmental, social, and governance, particularly in mobilizing corporations to advance the Social Agenda with focus on diversity, equity, and inclusion. Her career includes decades working for the United Nations, UNICEF, the Global Fund to Fight

AIDS, and the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR). She has participated in the creation and launch of global frameworks such as the Sustainable Development Goals and Product (RED).

In partnership with Brent Hoberman's Founders Factory, Claudia is a partner of Hispanic Star Ventures, a $20 million fund investing seed and pre-seed funding to U.S. Hispanic entrepreneurs bringing innovation to consumer goods and healthcare.

Claudia serves in the boards of Canoo and is a board nominee of Chobani, along with several non-for-profit organizations. She has two children, enjoys sports with her husband, and speaks six languages.

Dia
Simms

  • ADVISORY TEAM
  • Linkedin

Dia serves as the CEO of Lobos 1707 Tequila & Mezcal, which she leads alongside Founder Diego Osorio with early backing by sports and cultural icon LeBron James. In May 2021, Dia co-founded Pronghorn, a 10-year initiative to drive diversity, equity, and inclusion in the spirits industry. Backed by the world's leading spirits producer, Diageo, Pronghorn seeks to help participants within the Black community achieve the goal of generating $2 billion in economic value in the U.S.

Dia spent almost fifteen years working alongside Sean "Diddy" Combs at Combs Enterprises, starting in 2005 as his executive assistant. She grew within the company while building multiple brands and businesses under the Combs Enterprises umbrella. In 2017, Dia was named President of Combs Enterprises, making her the first president in the company's thirty-year history other than Sean Combs himself. In her role, she oversaw multi-billion-dollar brands under the Combs empire, including CÎROC Ultra-Premium Vodka, Blue Flame

Agency, AQUAhydrate, Bad Boy Entertainment, Sean John, and Revolt TV. She also led the transformation of CÎROC Ultra-Premium Vodka taking the previously unprofitable brand to a $2 billion retail value.

In 2017 and 2018, Dia was listed as "One of the Most Powerful Executives in the Industry" by Billboard, and made Ebony's "Power 100" list. In April of 2018 she was an honoree of the Matrix Awards from New York Women In Communications, as well as named to the 2018 Ad Age "Women to Watch" list and Variety's "Power of Women New York Impact List."

Dia serves on the board for the Maryland organization THREAD, which provides underprivileged Baltimore-area students resources to help foster their academic advancement and personal growth. She often speaks on the importance of diverse leadership and accelerated brand building. Dia resides in Georgia with her husband, daughter, and dog.
